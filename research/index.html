Alexander Franks

My methodological interests are broad, but involve covariance estimation, models for non-ignorable missing data, errors-in-variables models, data integration and methods for the analysis of spatio-temporal data. I am involved in many collaborations most of which involve the analysis of proteomic or metabolomic data. I have also recently been working on complex sports analytics with player-tracking data. See my research highlights below or download my CV.
